# Kazo Archdeaconry Children's Ministry (KACM)

> "Let the little children come to me..." — Matthew 19:14

Official website for **Kazo Archdeaconry Children's Ministry (KACM)**, a ministry of the
Church of Uganda under the **Namirembe Diocese**, headquartered at Kazo.

## 🌍 About

KACM is dedicated to nurturing, empowering, and supporting children through:
- Christian values and spiritual growth
- Charitable outreach programs
- Educational sponsorship and school fees funding
- Community events and activities

## ✨ Features

- 📅 **Events** — Browse past and upcoming ministry events
- 🤝 **Charity Activities** — Highlights of ongoing and completed outreach work
- 💛 **Donate** — Support a child's education or charitable cause
- 📰 **News & Updates** — Latest from the ministry
- 📬 **Contact** — Get in touch with the KACM team
