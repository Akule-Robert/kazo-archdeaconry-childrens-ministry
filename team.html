Our Team

Home > Our Team

"Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor." — Ecclesiastes 4:9

KACM is led by a dedicated team of spiritual leaders, coordinators, and community volunteers who give their time and passion to serve the children of Kazo Archdeaconry.

Leadership

Rt. Rev. John S. Kaggwa
Bishop, Namirembe Diocese

The Rt. Rev. John S. Kaggwa provides spiritual oversight and direction for the entire Namirembe Diocese, under which KACM operates. His leadership and vision for children's ministry have been foundational to KACM's growth.

Ven. Canon David M. Tumusiime
Archdeacon, Kazo Archdeaconry

Ven. Canon David Tumusiime serves as the Archdeacon of Kazo, providing spiritual leadership across all parishes and championing the cause of children's welfare throughout the Archdeaconry.

Rev. Canon Grace N. Birungi
KACM Rev. Coordinator

Rev. Canon Grace Birungi coordinates all KACM activities across parishes, working tirelessly to ensure every programme aligns with our mission of nurturing children in the love of Christ.

Mr. Stephen K. Rwabwogo
Council Coordinator

Mr. Stephen Rwabwogo leads the KACM Council, ensuring effective governance, strategic planning, and accountability across all ministry operations and partnerships.

Executive Members

Mrs. Agnes K. Nantume
Secretary

Oversees all administrative records, meeting minutes, and official correspondence for KACM.

Mr. Robert M. Byaruhanga
Treasurer

Manages KACM's finances with transparency, ensuring every donation is stewarded faithfully.

Mrs. Florence A. Kansiime
Programs Coordinator

Leads the design and implementation of all child-focused programmes across the Archdeaconry.

Mr. Patrick K. Turyamureeba
Sponsorship Lead

Coordinates the Sponsor a Child programme, matching children in need with generous sponsors worldwide.
