From Our Sponsors

Hear from those who give — and the joy they find in changing a child's life.

Sarah M.
United Kingdom

"Sponsoring a child through KACM has been one of the most rewarding decisions of my life. I receive regular updates and see the direct impact of my support on my sponsored child's education and wellbeing. The transparency and personal connection with KACM is unlike any other organisation I've supported."

David K.
United States

"I started sponsoring two children through KACM three years ago, and the transformation I've witnessed is incredible. From struggling to afford basic school supplies to now ranking among the top students — these children just needed someone to believe in them. KACM's model of community-based sponsorship means every dollar truly reaches the child."

Rebecca T.
Australia

"What drew me to KACM was their holistic approach — they don't just pay school fees; they invest in the child's spiritual growth, nutrition, and emotional wellbeing. The letters I receive from my sponsored child are treasures. I visited Kazo last year and saw firsthand the love and dedication of the team. Truly inspiring."

From Sponsored Children

In their own words — how sponsorship has changed their lives.

Peter
Senior 3, Kazo Parish

"Before KACM, I could not afford school fees. My mother tried her best, but it was never enough. Now I am in Senior 3, and I dream of becoming a doctor. KACM gave me hope when I had none. I thank God for my sponsor every day."

Agnes
Primary 6, Burunga Parish

"I love school. When my father passed away, I thought my education was over. But KACM found me a sponsor, and now I have books, a uniform, and food at school. I want to become a teacher so I can help other children like me."

Brian
Senior 5, Rwamuranda Parish

"The Resource Centre changed everything for me. I had never touched a computer before. Now I am learning ICT skills that will help me in university and beyond. KACM showed me that being from a village doesn't limit my future."

From Our Communities

Parents, local leaders, and community members share the impact of KACM.

Mrs. Jane Kyomugisha
Parent, Kazo

"As a widow with five children, I was overwhelmed. KACM not only sponsors two of my children's education but also taught me farming techniques through Farm to Fork. Now I can feed my family and even sell surplus vegetables. KACM didn't just help my children — they helped our whole family stand on our own feet."

Mr. Charles Tumusiime
LC1 Chairman, Burunga

"KACM has brought real change to our village. The community outreaches bring healthcare and food to families who would otherwise go without. Their child protection sensitisation has reduced cases of child abuse in our area. As a local leader, I fully endorse their work — they are a true partner to our community."

Mrs. Doreen Ninsiima
Sunday School Teacher, Kanyaryeru

"Teaching Sunday school used to be a challenge — we had no materials, no training. KACM equipped us with teaching guides, translated resources in Runyakitara, and training workshops. Now our children are more engaged than ever. They lead worship, they pray boldly, and they are growing in faith. It is a joy to serve."
